About us

We are an international multicultural Christian charitable ministry ….

Vision

To see people come to a full knowledge of Jesus Christ as lord and saviour reaching out to the world with the love of God.

Our Mission

To proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ through prayer, evangelism, healing, deliverance and discipleship.

History

Divine Healing Church was birthed on 12/11/2015 by the senior pastor,
Mrs Lydia Kuganja Bikkondo, after being spoken to by the Lord to open a hospital for healing his people.

“ While still living in Uganda,
the Lord spoke to me and instructed that I should open up a hospital that he may heal his people. So, I waited on the lord for the opportune time. When I came to the United Kingdom, the Lord once again spoke to me about the hospital, this time revealing to me where I should go. It was to Edmonton that’ I was sent on this vision and dreams. After seeing many demonstrations of his power, he confirmed to me that it was the right time to start.
